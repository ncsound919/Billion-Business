# Billion Dollar Acquisition Trap — Master Agenda

## The Mission
Build a portfolio of independent, medium-coupled AI ventures — each a standalone **billion-dollar threat** — structured so that when one is acquired, competitors are forced into a bidding war for the rest.

## Dual Architecture
The portfolio has two layers:

### Layer 1: For-Profit Moonshots (Acquisition Pressure)
High-valuation AI ventures designed to trigger bidding wars between NVIDIA, Microsoft, Google, Palantir, and Pharma acquirers.

### Layer 2: Non-Profit + Community Impact (Mission Anchor)
UL2 (Uplift Lab) as the non-profit sector — aiding the Black community through AI education, access, and economic empowerment. Uplift-Venture + AetherDesk as low-cost vehicles serving small businesses.

This dual structure creates a **moat that can't be acquired away** — the for-profit assets attract acquisition pressure, while the community layer ensures the mission survives any exit.

## Overlay365 Structure
Overlay365 is the parent company housing all ventures. **25% of all Overlay365 equity/revenue flows to UL2 (Uplift Lab)** — the non-profit community platform. This means:
- Any acquisition of Overlay365 or its subsidiaries carries a 25% non-profit mandate
- Acquiring the for-profit layer is only possible if the acquirer honors the UL2 funding stream
- Creates a structural defense: hostile acquirers face reputational damage for jeopardizing Black community funding
- UL2's non-profit status + 25% claim makes the portfolio politically expensive to dismantle
